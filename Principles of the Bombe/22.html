Contradictions

The eagle-eyed reader may have noticed the problem we've run into.

We started off by assuming an initial scrambler position, and that α = b.

Based on these assumptions we just concluded that α = h.

The assumption α = b tells us that a is steckered to b, but the conclusion α = h tells us that a is instead steckered to h!

This is problematic, as there is only one a socket in the plugboard, so a can only be steckered to one other letter. In other words, the stecker variable α cannot be equal to multiple values simultaneously.

So what gives? Did we do something wrong?
