The Scramblers

The M3 Enigma machine (which the British Bombe aimed specifically to crack) had three scrambler slots, as well as a special slot for a reflector (see Figure 2.4). A reflector is similar to a scrambler, except it only has contacts on one side, and does not rotate during the course of encrypting a message. The contacts on this one side are, again, electronically paired up in an unpredictable manner.
