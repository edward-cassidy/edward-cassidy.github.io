It is very important to note that the encryption performed by an Enigma machine is reciprocal.

That means if we were to press e instead of b when at RSP 0, then the fast rotor would rotate so that we are now at RSP 1, then current would flow through the Enigma mechanism exactly in reverse compared to before, and we would receive an output of b.
