About this Site

This website aims to be a comprehensive guide to understanding the underlying principles behind the bombe's design.

We explain the transformation of these principles into the electromechanical mechanism that drives the bombe, in the form of interactive circuit-diagrams outlining the internal wiring.

Due to the complexity of the Turing-Welchman Bombe, we will be considering a reduced bombe for the course of this guide. The original Enigma machine and Bombe uses all 26 letters of the alphabet, however in our examples, we will only use the first 8 for simplicity. That being said, the principles discussed directly translate to the operation of the full bombe.

The guide is complete with a fully-functional Bombe simulator built from those same circuit-diagrams.

We hope you enjoy!
