Operation: The Plugboard

After the fast scrambler (and possibly one or both of the other scramblers) have rotated, an electrical current is sent through the a wire to the plugboard.

If there is no cable attached to the a socket in the plugboard, then the a wire simply continues onto the scramblers.

Suppose instead that there is a stecker cable plugged in between the a socket and the b socket. In this case, power travels along this stecker cable and into the b wire, before being sent to the scramblers. Effectively, the a keypress has been swapped to a b keypress.
