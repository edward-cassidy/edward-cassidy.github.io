The Expanded Menu Diagram

The expanded menu diagram provides more detail about what is actually happening when the plaintext is encoded to the ciphertext.

Recall how the line labelled 1 between b and e in the simple menu diagram means that b was encoded to e in the crib when the scramblers were in relative position (RSP) 1.
