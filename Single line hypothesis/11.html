The Scramblers

In the next page we will connect the rest of the scramblers corresponding each of the edges in the menu on the cycle.

It's important to remember that each of the scrambler, will need to be in their corresponding relative scrambler position, as denoted by the labels on the edges of the menu.

Therefore, a scrambler with a RSP of 4 will be 3 positions ahead of a scrambler with RSP 1.
