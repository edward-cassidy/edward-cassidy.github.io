We will call the Greek letter variables we have created "stecker variables".

There are many times when we want to refer to, for example, the "stecker partner of b". So to simplify matters we give the stecker partner of b a label, in this case the Greek letter beta, β. Saying that "β = a" is synonymous with saying "b is steckered to a".
