Assumptions and Implications

We will first clarify what information and resources we have access to along with the crib:

Let’s say we know which scramblers were used to encode the crib (for example scramblers I, II, IV, and reflector B), and the order in which they were installed in the Enigma machine.

Let's also say we have physical copies of these scramblers, and that we can manually pass a character through them to see an output.

Lets say we don’t, however, know their initial positions. Therefore, as discussed before, we will need to search through every possible initial scrambler position.
