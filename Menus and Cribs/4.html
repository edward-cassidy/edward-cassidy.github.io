Relative Scrambler Positions (RSPs)

Even though we don't know the absolute scrambler positions at any point during the crib's encyption, we can safely talk about relative scrambler positions.

That is, if we label the initial scrambler position as relative scrambler position 0, then when the i'th letter of the crib is encoded, the scramblers are in relative scrambler position i, meaning that the fast scrambler has rotated i steps since relative scrambler position 0.
