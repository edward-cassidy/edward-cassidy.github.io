Wrapping Up

The addition of the diagonal board marks the end of the design of the Bombe, and therefore the end of this guide.

We highly recommend you try out our interactive Bombe simulator. It is complete with a menu generator, which enables you to generate ciphertext from A-H plaintext, visualise its menu, and then automatically set up the Bombe ready to crack it (or why not try setting up the Bombe to crack it yourself!).

The Bombe can then be used to search for a possibly valid initial rotor position, as well as letting you explore the circuit by powering wires on and observing to which wires current reaches.
